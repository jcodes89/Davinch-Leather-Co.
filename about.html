About Us

Davinch Leather Co. was started with one goal in mind, that is to provide a product that will withstand the daily wear and tear that life throws at you. Jeremy Sluder has been a leather artisan for six plus years. The Davinch Leather brand is influenced from Japanese and European style leather crafting and design.

Each item is crafted by hand which allows the artisan to focus on every detail of the build. There is no mass production here at Davinch Leather Co. We know each and every person has there individual style, so why not have a leather good that you can create to fit that style. Thank you for taking the time to find this page and please feel free to reach out if you have any questions.
