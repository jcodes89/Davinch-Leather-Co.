Hand Crafted Leather Goods

Each leather good item is created using age old methods that will withstand the test of time!

The Process

Step one

Think

  • Think of the leather good item you would like to create.
  • Think of any special features you would like to add.
  • Use contact form on custom work page to submit a request.

Step two

Design

  • The design process begins with a sketch
  • During this time the artisan and the client will be in communication to make sure every detail is perfect.
  • During this process, client is able to customize every aspect of the item. Including leather type, thread color, edge finish.

Step three

Create

  • Artisan now takes previous paper design and cuts out each leather piece by hand.
  • Leather item is meticulously glued together.
  • Once leather good item is glued, an artisan stitches the leather together using a method called saddle stitching.
  • Edge finish is then applied.

Step four

Enjoy!

  • Client recieves final product.
  • Time to enjoy your one of a kind custom leather piece.
